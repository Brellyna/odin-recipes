Cappuccino

Description

How to make cappuccino at home! This popular espresso drink is creamy with just the right touch of foamed milk.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Coffee
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Make the espresso using an espresso machine, espresso maker, or Aeropress
  2. Steam the milk and get it nice and foamy.
  3. Pour the frothed milk into the espresso and serve! Its nice to use a latte cup, which holds the perfect amount.
